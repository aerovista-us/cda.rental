FAQ

Practical answers before you submit — nothing replaces the approval email and rental agreement.

Is this official Ironman lodging?

No. This is independent private lodging near Ironman weekend activity. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by IRONMAN® or any race organizer.

How many guests can stay?

The home is presented for about 6–8 guests (1320 sqft, 3 bed / 2 bath). Larger groups may be considered after review of sleeping arrangements, parking, and stay purpose.

Is there bike storage?

Bike and gear staging uses designated areas agreed at booking — not hallways or bedrooms unless approved. Details are confirmed after approval.

How does payment work?

Submitting a request costs nothing. After approval, you receive a secure link for the reserve deposit. Balance and security deposit handling are spelled out before you pay.

Is the deposit refundable?

Refund and cancellation terms depend on your rental agreement and timing — they are provided clearly before you commit funds.

When do I get the address?

The exact address and arrival instructions are shared only after the stay is reviewed and approved, required agreement steps are complete, and reservation payment milestones are met.

Can I book only part of the date range?

Yes. Select any pattern of nights June 12–23 on the calendar; Jun 18–21 moves as one four-night peak block.

Are pets allowed?

Not unless approved in writing before arrival. Default is no pets.

Is parking available?

Parking is reviewed with your vehicle count before approval so the plan works for your crew.

After you submit

  • Who replies: The host reviews each request and replies to the email address you provide.
  • When: Typically within one business day, often sooner during race season.
  • Verification: Guest list, agreement, and basic verification may be required for approved stays.
  • Address: Exact address and arrival instructions are shared only after approval, signed agreement as required, and payment steps toward the reservation are complete.
