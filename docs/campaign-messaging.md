# Campaign messaging (CDA Race Week Basecamp)

Reference for ads, email, and rental-group posts. On-site copy lives in `index.html`; this doc captures the positioning framework.

## Positioning

Do **not** lead with “vacation rental.” Lead with **keeping the crew together on race week** — a **private home base** for the whole race-week crew.

## Emotional pain points (creative angles)

- Hotels are expensive during race week  
- Rooms get split across bookings  
- Race gear is awkward in hotel rooms  
- Pre-race food is harder without a kitchen  
- Parking is stressful  
- Family/support coordination gets messy when people are scattered  
- Athletes need quiet, sleep, food, and routine  

## Core offer

**A private home base for the whole race-week crew** — 3BR / 2BA Coeur d’Alene, June window as published on the site.

## Audience hooks (short lines)

| Audience | Message |
|----------|---------|
| Athlete + support crew | One home base for sleep, food, gear, and recovery. |
| Family traveling with athlete | Keep everyone together instead of splitting across hotel rooms. |
| Last-minute lodging searchers | Private CDA race-week stay still accepting date requests. |
| Regional triathlon crowd | Coming to CDA for race weekend? Request a private 3BR home base. |
