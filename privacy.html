Privacy

This page describes how information submitted through the Heart of Awl Basecamp marketing site is handled.

Booking requests

When you submit the booking request form, your message is sent to our team through Formspree, a third-party form processor. Formspree’s handling of data is governed by their policies at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy. We use Formspree only to receive and respond to stay inquiries.

What we collect

  • Information you type into the form (for example name, email, phone, dates, guest count, and notes).
  • Technical data that hosting providers and Formspree may log (such as IP address or browser type) as described in their respective policies.

How we use it

We use inquiry details to evaluate whether the stay is a good fit, communicate about availability and next steps, and—if you proceed—support scheduling, agreements, and deposits as discussed with you directly.

Retention

We retain inquiry and booking-related correspondence only as long as needed for those purposes or as required by law. You may ask us to delete or correct submitted information by replying to any message we send you about your request.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. Do not submit personal information if you are under 13.

We may update this page occasionally. The “last updated” note below reflects the latest revision.
